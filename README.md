# anamnesis.fm

*A time traveling radio.*

Listen in at [anamnesis.fm](https://anamnesis.fm).

---

The radio aggregates content from these [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/) collections:

- [Old Time Radio](https://archive.org/details/oldtimeradio) - Golden age radio dramas, news, comedy, and music from the 1920s-1960s
- [Radio Programs](https://archive.org/details/radioprograms) - Massive collection of radio show recordings
- [Live Music Archive (etree)](https://archive.org/details/etree) - Trade-friendly live concert recordings
- [Hip Hop Radio Archive](https://archive.org/details/hiphopradioarchive) - Classic hip-hop radio recordings and mix shows
- [Audio Music Collection](https://archive.org/details/audio_music) - Music, arts, and culture broadcasts
- [Open Source Audio](https://archive.org/details/opensource_audio) - Community-contributed audio recordings
- [Voice of America Newscasts](https://archive.org/details/VOANewscasts) - 86,000+ VOA news broadcasts from around the world
- [Stretch Armstrong & Bobbito Show](https://archive.org/search?query=Stretch+Armstrong+Bobbito) - Legendary NYC hip-hop radio
- [BBC Radio Recordings](https://archive.org/search?query=BBC+radio) - British Broadcasting Corporation archives
- [NPR Programs](https://archive.org/search?query=NPR+radio) - National Public Radio content
- [Radio Mystery Theater](https://archive.org/search?query=CBS+Radio+Mystery+Theater) - CBS classic mystery dramas
- [The Shadow](https://archive.org/search?query=The+Shadow+radio) - Classic crime-fighting radio drama
- [Dragnet](https://archive.org/search?query=Dragnet+radio) - Police procedural radio series
- [War of the Worlds (1938)](https://archive.org/details/OrsonWellesMrBruns) - Orson Welles' famous broadcast
- [Grateful Dead Live](https://archive.org/details/GratefulDead) - Extensive live show archive
- [WFMU](https://archive.org/search?query=WFMU) - Freeform radio from New Jersey
- [KEXP Live Sessions](https://archive.org/search?query=KEXP) - Seattle's legendary music station
